Architecture Overview

Architectural Position

Contemporary AI stacks optimize model capability, memory, retrieval, and tool use. Certor™ addresses a different layer: whether execution should be authorized at all.

Independent Layer

Certor is positioned outside the model, acting as a separate authorization boundary rather than an instruction embedded within model behavior.

Execution Control

The system focuses on the execution layer, where actions are allowed, deferred, or blocked before downstream activity occurs.

Model-Agnostic Design

The architecture is intended to remain portable across models, agents, adapters, and operational environments.
