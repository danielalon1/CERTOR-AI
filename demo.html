What the Demo Is Meant to Show

The demonstration is not centered on model output alone. It is centered on the control decision that precedes downstream execution.

Request Intake

A request enters the flow and is evaluated before execution rights are granted.

Authorization Decision

The system returns a decision such as permit, defer, or block at the execution boundary.

Controlled Outcome

Downstream action proceeds only when the authorization condition is satisfied.
