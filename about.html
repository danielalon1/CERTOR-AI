About Certor™

Certor™ is an architectural authority layer for AI systems, built around a simple but critical principle: authorization must precede execution.

Vision

To establish a new standard for AI systems where execution is not driven solely by model output, but governed by an independent authority layer.

Approach

Certor™ introduces a model-independent control layer that determines whether an action is allowed before it is executed, creating a clear separation between decision and execution.

Intellectual Property & Patent Status

The Certor™ architecture and its underlying concepts are currently under ongoing patent development and protection processes, forming part of an evolving intellectual property portfolio.

All intellectual property related to Certor™ is owned by Daniel Alon, the system architect and originator of the "Authority Before Execution" model.

The structure, logic, and implementation approach of Certor™ represent a foundational shift in how AI systems are governed at the execution layer.
