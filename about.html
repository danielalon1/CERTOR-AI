About Certor™

Certor™ is built around a simple architectural principle: authorization must precede execution.

Vision

To establish an authority layer for AI systems that governs whether actions are allowed before execution occurs.

Approach

Model-independent, architecture-first, and focused on execution boundaries rather than prompt-only control.
