About Certor

Certor™ is an architectural initiative focused on authorization at the execution layer of AI systems.

Architectural Focus

Certor is designed as a distinct layer positioned before execution, rather than embedded within model behavior.

Design Principle

Models may generate outputs, but execution requires an additional, independent decision layer.

Operational Direction

Development is oriented toward real authorization workflows, execution-bound control, and traceable decisions.

Research Direction

The Certor research line progresses through: concept → architecture → execution validation.

The project is currently under patent process, and all intellectual property belongs to Daniel Alon.
