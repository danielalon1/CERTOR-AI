About Certor™

An authorization architecture for AI execution.

Authority is not a feature. It is a layer.

Certor™ is an architecture initiative focused on authorization at the execution layer of AI systems. Its central premise is that execution should be subject to an independent authorization boundary, rather than relying solely on model output or application logic.

Architectural Focus

Certor is designed as a distinct layer positioned before execution rather than embedded within model behavior.

Design Principle

Models may generate outputs, but execution requires an additional, independent decision layer.

Operational Direction

Development is oriented toward real authorization workflows, execution-bound control, and traceable decisions.

Research Direction

The Certor research line progresses through three layers: foundational framing of the authorization problem, architectural specification, and MVP-level validation at the execution boundary.

Intellectual Property

Certor™ and its underlying architectural concepts are part of an ongoing intellectual property and patent development process. Related intellectual property is owned by Daniel Alon.
